Kaede Ninpouchou

Art: Nakano Yayoi Story: Yoshimura An Serialization: Nakayoshi

The Kageuras are a family of hermit ninjas. But when an old family friend shows up and throws them out of their home, they find themselves lost in modern society, having to fight off the goons of the man that threw them out of their original home.

Totsuzen Parallel!

Art & Story: Nakano Yayoi Serialization: RunRun

Truly, this is Furry Isekai™! Nanako and friends fall through a magic mirror and swap places with versions of themselves from a world where anthropomorphic animals take the place of humans. Their task is simply to find a way home.
